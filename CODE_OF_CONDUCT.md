# Code of Conduct

We want Camel AI to be a friendly, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

**Be respectful.** No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
Assume good faith, give constructive feedback, and keep discussion focused on the
project.

**Use it responsibly.** Camel AI automates real software and logins. Only automate
accounts and systems you're authorized to use.

Violations may be reported to the maintainer via GitHub. Maintainers may remove
comments, commits, and contributions that violate this code, and may ban
repeat offenders.

Adapted in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
